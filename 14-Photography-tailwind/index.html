    About Founder

    Sarah Thompson is an award-winning photographer with over 15 years of experience in the industry. Her passion for capturing moments began during her travels across Europe, where she developed a unique perspective on light and composition. Sarah founded The Photography to share her knowledge and help aspiring photographers develop their skills. Her work has been featured in National Geographic and The New York Times, and she continues to inspire through her innovative teaching methods and breathtaking imagery.
